Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • 2 Tbs lemon juice
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp mustard
    Dijon or spicy brown are both good
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbs nutritional yeast

Yields enough for ~4-5 salads.

Instructions

  1. Mix everything together.
    I find it’s convenient to mix in a liquid measuring cup so it’s easier to pour.
  2. Enjoy!
