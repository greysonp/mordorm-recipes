Cornbread

Ingredients

Dry

  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow corn meal
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder

Wet

  • 1 1/4 cup non-dairy milk
  • 1/3 cup neutral oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 F.
  2. Mix all of the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the same bowl and stir to combine.
    No need to mix them in a separate bowl ahead of time.
  4. Pour the mix into an 8x8" square pan, or a 9" round cake pan.
  5. Cook for 20-25 minutes. Cornbread is done when a toothpick comes out clean and the edges are beginning to brown.
  6. Enjoy!
