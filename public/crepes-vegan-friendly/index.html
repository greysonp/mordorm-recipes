Ingredients

  • 120 g flour
  • 8 oz milk
    Can be plant-based
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 Tbs flax meal
    Or 2 eggs for non-vegan option
  • 1 Tbs butter
    Vegan or non-vegan

Instructions

  1. Gather wet and dry ingredients, get a medium-sized mixing bowl, and two small bowls to melt butter + soak flaxseed meal.
  2. Measure 120 g of flour in a medium-sized mixing bowl.
  3. Add to this 8 oz of your milk of choice (I used oat milk).
  4. Add a teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  5. Add a teaspoon of sugar.
  6. Add 1 tablespoon of your butter of choice (I used salted butter).
  7. Add 2 tablespoons of flaxseed meal, soaked in 3 oz of water OR add two large eggs.
  8. Whisk together until combined and runny.
  9. Heat a pan to high heat, butter.
  10. Add batter (about 1/3 cup) to hot pan, spread evenly around the pan using gravity + a spoon to make a thin crepe.
  11. Flip after a few minutes, remove from pan after 30 seconds. Repeat until batter is gone (make about 6 crepes).
  12. Serve with your favorite toppings (butter and sugar, whipped creme and berries, lemon curd, etc), and tea.
