Ingredients

Mole

  • 7 dried chiles, stemmed and seeded
  • 1 1/2 cups hot water
  • 2 (6-inch) corn tortillas, or a handful of tortilla chips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 medium onions, chopped
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 Tbs smooth peanut butter
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 3/4 cups vegetable stock
  • 3 oz of chocolate
    Mexican chocolate preferred, but bittersweet also works

Enchiladas

  • 1 12-oz package of Impossible meat
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 package of vegan cheese
  • 1 15-oz can of refried beans
  • 4 (8-inch) flour tortillas

Crema

  • 1 cup raw cashews
  • 1/2 earl grey tea
  • 1 sprig of mint
  • Lemon juice

Fried Plantains

  • Ripe plantains
  • Vegan butter

Instructions

Mole

  1. Stem and de-seed your chiles. Place them in the hot water.

Soak for 15 minutes.

  1. Toast your corn tortillas in a dry skillet. Tearn into pieces and set aside.
  2. Add olive oil to the pan and set to medium heat.
  3. Chop the garlic and onions.
  4. Cook onions with some salt for 3 min, or until translucent.
  5. Add garlic and cook for an additional 2 min.
  6. In a blender, combine the onions+garlic, chiles, tortillas, peanut butter, oregano, and vegetable stock. Blend until smooth.
  7. Transfer sauce to a medium saute pan and bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  8. Stir in the chocolate until melted.
  9. Season with salt and pepper.

Enchiladas

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Chop your onions and garlic.
  3. Add some oil to a pan on medium heat.
  4. Add the onions and cook for around 2 min.
  5. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Add the Impossible meat stir until thoroughly cooked.
  7. Assemble your echiladas (see below) and place in your baking dish(es).
  8. Cover with mole sauce.
  9. Place a layer of cheese on top.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  11. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
  12. After plating, drizzle your crema on top.

Assembling an Enchilada

  1. Warm a tortilla in the microwave for 10 seconds.
  2. Apply a thin layer of refried beans.
  3. Apply some cheese.
  4. Apply a quarter of your prepared filling.
  5. Roll into a tube and place in your baking dish.

Crema

  1. Place cashews in boiling water.

Soak for 5 minutes.

  1. Prepare the earl grey tea.
  2. Blend all incredients in a blender.

Fried Plantains

  1. Slice your plantains diaganoally, to maximize surface area.
  2. Melt some vegan butter in a pan.
  3. Cook plantains in the butter until each side is golden.
